galmweefsel

2017. Meditative chant looping and reverberating through the space. The speaker and the net hanging from the first floor balcony support the sound trying to resonate visually.

extra muros

2016. An installation occupying a vacant building plot in the Rasphuisstraat in Ghent. Two speakers and a big mirror were hoisted up on the metal beams between the two neighbouring buildings. I filled the street with loud noisy improvised singing with soundeffects. The place where I was sitting was not visible from the street except when looking from a specific spot though the mirror.

2015. Voorkamer in Lier gave our class the opportunity to make in situ works at their impressive 17th century barn. I wanted to make something not just in touch with the building but also with our presence there . Using materials found on the spot, I made a bed hanging from the ceiling beams and a mattress made from bubble wrap, dried grass and tape. I slept there for three nights.
